Lasagna

Description

If you love a flavoursome, cheesy slice of lasagna then look no further, as this recipe has been passed down by generation after generation of the most authentic Italian Nonnas, resulting in a modern take on a classic recipe which tastes so good, it would make even Richard Dawson believe that God truly does exist.

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 50g prosciutto, roughly chopped
  • 250g pack fresh lasagna sheets
  • 1 celery stick, finely chopped
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped
  • 700g minced beef
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tin of chopped tomatoes
  • 150ml beef stock
  • 1 tbsp fresh oregano, finely chopped
  • small bunch fresh basil leaves
  • 60g unsalted butter
  • 60g plain flour
  • 700ml whole milk
  • 80g parmesan, finely grated
  • pinch of nutmeg
